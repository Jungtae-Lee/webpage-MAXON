Modularization

What is Modularization?

Modularization is an execution approach for design, procurement, contracting and construction that shifts site construction hours away from the site

Includes skids, pre-assemblies, entire process structures, machines, and other structures including bridges

When is Modularization effective?

- Remote site access

- Severe site weather constraints

- Schedule-driven improvement

- Limited availability of regional skilled labor/imported construction labor/man camps

- Extensive Factory Acceptance Testing(FAT) desired

- High module potential/repeatable facility construction

- High density piping areas
